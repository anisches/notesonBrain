# Research Questions

## Consciousness

- What counts as a neural correlate of consciousness rather than a prerequisite or consequence?
- Where do global workspace, recurrent processing, predictive processing, and IIT agree or conflict?
- Which experiments distinguish awareness from reportability?

## Agency And Free Will

- What does readiness potential actually show?
- Are Libet-style findings about conscious will, motor preparation, or measurement framing?
- How do action selection and post-hoc rationalization interact?

## Decision-Making

- How does the brain represent uncertainty and confidence?
- What does dopamine encode across reward, salience, and prediction error?
- What changes when decisions are made under time pressure?

## Self

- Is the self best treated as a model, process, narrative, control system, or illusion?
- How do interoception and body ownership contribute to the sense of self?
